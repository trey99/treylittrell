PSF phishing spamming flaming

Phishing - Sending fake emails to deceive someone into giving you their passwords or credit card information. Flaming – An insulting interaction between people on the internet often involving profanity. Spamming – Sending messages to a large number of people over the internet. Phishing and spamming are similar in that they in involve sending out emails to get something from the recipient of the message.
